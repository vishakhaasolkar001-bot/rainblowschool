About Us

A Place Where Children Truly Belong

Rainbow Blow Child School is a vibrant, nature-centred primary school built on the belief that every child flourishes when learning feels like play. Our lush green campus is designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

With a rich garden programme, colourful classrooms, and dedicated educators, we nurture the whole child — head, heart, and hands.

  • Child-centred, play-based curriculum
  • Beautiful garden learning spaces
  • Small class sizes — maximum 20 per class
  • Multilingual environment
  • Art, music & sport every day
Our Programs

Learning Through Every Colour

From gardening to science, art to sport — every programme is designed to let children shine.

🌱

Garden Science

Children grow their own vegetables and flowers, learning biology and ecology hands-on in our 3-acre school garden.

🎨

Creative Arts

Painting, sculpture, drama, and music — daily arts sessions foster self-expression and confidence in every child.

📚

Reading & Literacy

A joyful phonics and storytelling programme that turns every child into a confident, enthusiastic reader.

🏃

Outdoor Sports

From football to yoga, our expansive grounds give children daily outdoor activity for health and teamwork.

🔬

Curious Minds STEM

Fun experiments, building challenges, and coding games develop logical thinking from the earliest years.

🌍

World Languages

Songs, stories, and play in English, Hindi, and Marathi celebrate our multilingual community every day.
